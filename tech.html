What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

Margins borders and paddings are used in web design. We use them to put all of our content into and set peremeters around them to control how they look on a web page and where they sit. When we use them properly we can make all sorts of beautiful web pages.

Margins are the space outside of an elements border. they are the space between elements. kind of like the space between planets, space itself would be the margin.

Borders are boundary around the padding of an elements content. they seperate one element from anonther element. they can be used to seperate elements from eachother visually. also like space you could say borders are like the edge of a planets atmosphere.

Padding is the space between an elements content and border. and also like space the padding would be the atmosphere of a planet whereas the content would be the planet itself.

To sum it all up the difference between a margin, border and padding is their positioning relative to the content they surround. but they all work together to bring you a nice protective box.
